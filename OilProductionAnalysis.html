Energy Data Analysis Project¶

This project analyzes historical crude oil price trends (1861-2022) using the Kaggle Crude Oil Prices dataset.

  • Tools: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Dataset: Crude Oil Prices from Kaggle (crude-oil-prices.csv)
  • Goal: Identify and visualize price trends, changes, and patterns over time
